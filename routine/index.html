How it works

Routine is designed for the gap between generic programs and the messy reality of a normal week.

Why it matters

Consistency needs a plan that can bend.

Most people do not fail because they lack ambition. They fail because the plan stops matching the reality of their body, time, equipment, and confidence. Routine keeps the work practical.
